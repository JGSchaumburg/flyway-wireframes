Two refinements from Jun 15
entrance → decision node → platform
Journeys break at fare gates / decision points, not entrance-to-platform, so you don't re-capture the same wayfinding on every route. Acceptance = every decision point documented, not every pathway.
shortest-path on the pathways graph
Once you have the graph, routing is straightforward; OTP adds confusing failure modes with no in-station upside.
